Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods does your restaurant accept?

We accept cash, bank cards (Visa, MasterCard, Mir), and online payments via Kaspi Pay and Apple Pay.

Do I need to book a table in advance?

We recommend booking in advance, especially during evenings and weekends. You can make a reservation through our website or by phone.

Is it possible to host private events at your restaurant?

Yes, we have private dining areas suitable for events such as birthdays, corporate dinners, and celebrations. Please contact us in advance to discuss the details.

Do you offer takeaway or delivery services?

Absolutely! We offer both takeaway and city-wide delivery. You can place your order through our website or by phone.
